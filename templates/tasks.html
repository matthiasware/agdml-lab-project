Tasks

Implement a recommender system that predicts the rating for a given (user, item) tuple. Complete the (user, item) tuples in the file "qualifying.csv". You are free in the choice of your technique(s). Be creative! Write a project report (2-5 content pages) where you describe your algorithm, its performance, technical details, your score. In the report focus on the key features of your system.

Exam

Submit the project report alongside the code and your final score until 31.07.18 12:00 am via mail to:

matthias.mitterreiter@uni-jena.de
There will be a brief oral examination on the 03.08, where we discuss your approach. Your project report and code is the basis of the discussion.

Advice

You may start with literature research. If you have questions or get stuck, ask early. Focus on quality, not quantity but if you have time, hybrid-algorithms are the way to go. Understand the methods! Use cross-validation to test your implementation. Your final score will not directly influence the grading, however it may indicate a deficient implementation.

Have fun and happy coding!
